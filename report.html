Analysis of Usability and Accessibility Testing

According to the test, all the groups thought the quality of information about individual product,
completeness of order form was not satisfying.

However, they were very satisfied with the ease of navigation and reading texts and
were also very satisfied with the speed of pages displayed.

Most of the users who tested our website were satisfied with fun, entertainment value;
quality of information about ordering and delivery.

The groups participated in testing our website were satisfied overall website experience.
